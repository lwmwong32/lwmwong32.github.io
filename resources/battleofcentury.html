Pick your player and save the day! This game allows you to fight against the computer.
Choose your character.
Controls:
Dragon: Use arrow keys to move, up to fly, space to drop poison balls.
Dino: Use arrow keys to move, Up key to supercharge, space to target the enemy and click to launch
Dog: Use arrow keys to move, up key to jump, space to target and click to unleash an electric ball that can pass through walls.
Cat: Use arrow keys to move, up key to jump, space to use a sword strike.
Captain: Use arrow keys to move, down key to heal, space to target and click to unleash a cannonball.
